About Aeropress

The Aeropress, invented by Aerobie president Alan Adler, is a device used to steep and brew single cups of coffee. You steep coffee grinds and hot water in a plastic cylinder piece, then plunge the coffee through a filter piece directly into your coffee mug. The Aeropress is a great tool for making the perfect cup of coffee, as it’s focus is strength and smoothness. There are a few different ways to position your Aeropress for brewing, but this demonstration will show you my personal favorite way that I’ve found yields the best results! Step 1

  1. Preheat water to 190℉
  2. Extend your Aeropress so the rubber end is right above the '4'
  3. Grind 17 grams of coffee beans
  4. Stand the Aeropress up UPSIDE DOWN, put coffee grounds in

Top of Page

Step 2

  1. Pour heated water in so it just covers the grounds
  2. Stir in water, wait 30 seconds

Top of Page

Step 3

  1. Fill rest of Aeropress with water
  2. Put filter into filter piece, twist onto the Aeropress
  3. Wait 1 minute

Top of Page

Step 4

  1. Place the Aeropress on the opening of your mug, filter side facing down
  2. Slowly apply pressure to drain the Aeropress
  3. Add one and a half tablespoons of heavy whipping cream
  4. Stir and enjoy!
