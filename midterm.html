About Aeropress

The Aeropress, invented by Aerobie president Alan Adler, is a device used to steep and brew single cups of coffee. You steep coffee grinds and hot water in a plastic cylinder piece, then plunge the coffee through a filter piece directly into your coffee mug. The Aeropress is a great tool for making the perfect cup of coffee, as its focus is strength and smoothness. Step 1

  1. Preheat water to 190℉
  2. Extend your Aeropress so the rubber end is right above the '4'
  3. Grind 17 grams of coffee beans
  4. Stand the Aeropress up UPSIDE DOWN, put coffee grounds in
Top of Page

Step 2

  1. Pour heated water in so it just covers the grounds
  2. Stir in water, wait 30 seconds
  3. Rinse the stirring utensil
  4. Place a filter in the filter holder and run cold water through it
Top of Page

Step 3

  1. Fill rest of Aeropress with water
  2. Twist the filter holder onto the Aeropress
  3. Wait 1 minute
Top of Page

Step 4

  1. Flip the Aeropress onto the opening of your mug, filter side facing down
  2. Slowly apply pressure to drain the Aeropress, this should take about 30-40 seconds
  3. Add heavy whipping cream to taste
  4. Stir and enjoy!
